My passion is to build & ship data products that improves the lives of millions of people.

Ahmed leads the Data & AI organization at Hudhud Maps, where he’s building Saudi Arabia’s first homegrown mapping platform — from raw imagery to fully navigable maps — powered by geospatial AI and modern data infrastructure. With over a decade of experience in data and AI, Ahmed has built systems and led teams across MENA and the US, including at Careem, where he founded and scaled the company’s data infrastructure and ML platform teams. His work focuses on applying machine learning, geospatial intelligence, and large-scale data engineering to solve real-world problems affecting the lives of millions of people.
