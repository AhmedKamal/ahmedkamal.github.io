You can't pass properties or indexers by reference. So you can't do:
<pre><code>Foo(ref x.SomeProperty);</code></pre>
but you can do:
<pre><code>var tmp = x.SomeProperty;
Foo(ref tmp);
x.SomeProperty = tmp;</code></pre>
